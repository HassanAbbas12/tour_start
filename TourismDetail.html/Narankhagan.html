Naran Khagan


Naran Kaghan is the best place to visit in Pakistan, the weather of Naran in June and July is 20C. The cold weather of Naran always gives you the best feeling ever. Naran Kaghan is heaven for tourist. There are few best hotels in Naran Kaghan like Hotel Demanchi Naran, PTDC Motel Naran, and many others. From Islamabad airport, reached to Naran Bazar in 7 hours of drive and during the journey, you visit Abbottabad city, Mansehra Bypass, Balakot city and Kiwai. Kaghan Valley is very close to Naran Valley which is also the famous tourist hub. Kaghan Valley is the best place to enjoy cool weather in the summer season. Many foreigners and Pakistanis are spending their summer holidays in Kaghan Valley. Kaghan Valley is gorgeous and full of captivating views. People are very peaceful in nature.
